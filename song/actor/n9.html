Nayanthara

Nayanthara is considered among the most popular actor of Tamil , Telugu and Malayalam cinema. She is one of the highest paid actresses in South Indian cinema, according to various media reports. In March 2022, according to a report by The Times of India, Nayanthara became the highest-paid South Indian actress.
