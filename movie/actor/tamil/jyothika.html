Jyothika Movies


Jyothika Saravanan (born 18 October 1978) is an Indian actress and film producer who predominantly appears in Tamil films in addition to Telugu, Malayalam and Hindi films.Jyothika has received several accolades including a National Film Award, four Filmfare Awards South,[4] six Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and the Kalaimamani Award Jyothika is regarded as one of the most prolific actresses in Tamil cinema,and has been ranked among the best actresses of South India by the media.
